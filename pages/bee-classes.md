## What you'll learn

1. **How a colony works** — queen, workers, drones, seasonal cycles.
2. **Getting your first hive** — equipment choices, where to order bees, siting the hive.
3. **Installing a package or nuc** — step-by-step, with pictures.
4. **Inspecting a hive** — what to look at, what to ignore, how often.
5. **Pests and diseases** — varroa mites, small hive beetles, the honest truth about losses.
6. **Winter prep and spring buildup** — treatments, feeding, the critical six weeks either side of the cold.
7. **Field day** — visit a working apiary with a mentor. Smoke the bees. Hold a frame. Breathe.
