## Who can use it

Any member in good standing. First-year beekeepers are especially
encouraged — no point buying a $900 stainless steel drum for your first
30 lb of honey.

## How to reserve

1. Check the reservation calendar in the [members area]({{ '/members/' | relative_url }}).
2. Pick an open block (usually 2–3 days).
3. Contact the extractor steward (see [contacts]({{ '/contacts/' | relative_url }})) to confirm pickup and drop-off.

## Rules (the important bit)

- **Return it clean.** Food-safe clean. If the next member has to scrape
  crystallized honey off the cage, we will find you.
- **Suggested donation:** $10 per use, goes toward maintenance and
  eventual replacement.
- **Pickup window:** generally Saturday morning; drop-off within 72 hours
  unless arranged otherwise.
- **Transport it level.** Upside-down in a pickup bed is a bad time.

## What's included

- Radial extractor (4-frame manual, deep/medium convertible)
- Uncapping knife
- Straining bucket with double sieve
- A roll of paper towels, because we care
