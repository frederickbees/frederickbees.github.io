Membership

Join the nicest club in Frederick County.

Annual dues keep the lights on, the speakers coming, and the extractor spinning. Existing members can also renew here.

What membership includes

  • Access to all monthly meetings and guest speakers
  • Mentor pairing for first-year beekeepers
  • Use of the shared club extractor
  • Members-only resource library
  • Quarterly newsletter with local forage reports
  • Discounts from participating vendors

How to pay

Membership is $15 per year per household. Renewals run on a calendar year (January–December).

  1. Fill out the membership form (link below).
  2. Pay online via PayPal, or bring cash/check to the next meeting.
  3. We'll add you to the member directory and newsletter.
