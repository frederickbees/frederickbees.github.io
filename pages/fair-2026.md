## What we do at the Fair

- **Observation hive** — real live bees behind glass, a queen marked bright blue, visitors of every age staring at her like she invented electricity
- **Honey judging** — members submit jars for state-level competition
- **Honey sales** — jars from member apiaries, all local
- **Q&A** — somebody is always at the booth to answer "how do I get started?"

## Volunteer at the booth

We staff the booth in four-hour shifts. If you've ever wanted to talk
bees to strangers for half a day, this is your moment. Members in good
standing get free Fair admission for each shift worked.

- **Sign-up opens:** July 2026
- **Fair runs:** September 2026 (dates TBA)
- **Contact:** fair chair (see [contacts]({{ '/contacts/' | relative_url }}))

## Submit honey for judging

Entries open the first week of August. State judging categories include
extracted honey (light, amber, dark), comb honey, creamed honey, and
beeswax. Rules and entry forms come out with the Fair premium book.
