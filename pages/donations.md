## Where your donation goes

- **Bee School scholarships** for students and first-time beekeepers
- **Speaker fees** for visiting master beekeepers and researchers
- **Outreach supplies** — observation hive, kids' activities, printed materials
- **Club equipment** — extractor maintenance, meeting supplies

## Ways to give

1. **Online** — use the PayPal or Zelle links (coming soon).
2. **By check** — made out to *Frederick County Beekeeping Association* and
   mailed to the address on the [contacts page]({{ '/contacts/' | relative_url }}).
3. **In kind** — hive equipment, glass jars, printer paper. Email us first.

FCBA is a 501(c)(3); donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law.
