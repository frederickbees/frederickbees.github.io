Our mission

We keep bees so bees can keep keeping us.

FCBA is an organization of current and future beekeepers in the Frederick County, Maryland area. Our mission is to provide support and information to each other, to educate the public, and to advocate for the practice of responsible beekeeping.

We're part classroom, part support group, part swarm-response network — a few hundred neighbors (and several million tiny roommates) sharing what we've learned the hard way so you don't have to.

Emergency · Free service

Found a swarm?
Please don't spray.

Honey bees in a tight cluster on a branch, fence, or grill cover are usually docile and just looking for a new home. One of our volunteers can come collect them — free of charge — and give them a proper hive to live in.

Note: we only handle honey bees. For wasps, hornets, or yellow jackets, you'll want a licensed pest-control service.
